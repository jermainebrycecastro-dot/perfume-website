Bleu de Chanel EDP

Brand: Chanel

Type: Eau de Parfum

Released: 2014

Fragrance Notes

  • Top: Grapefruit, Lemon, Mint
  • Middle: Ginger, Nutmeg, Jasmine
  • Base: Incense, Sandalwood, Cedar

Description

Bleu de Chanel EDP is a woody-aromatic fragrance known for its clean, masculine, and versatile scent. It is often described as fresh yet deep, making it suitable for both daily wear and formal occasions.

Best For

  • Office and school
  • Date nights
  • All-season wear
